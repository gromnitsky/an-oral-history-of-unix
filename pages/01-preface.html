Preface

This is a compilation of interviews conducted by Professor Michael S. Mahoney. The interviews were recorded on tape during the summer-fall of 1989 & transcribed later by various people.

The original files from which this ebook was compiled, copyright et al. can be found on Professor's page at http://www.princeton.edu/~hos/Mahoney/unixhistory .

If you want to fix typos, add cover art, etc, open issues or send me a pull request at https://github.com/gromnitsky/an-oral-history-of-unix .
